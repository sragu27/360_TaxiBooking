Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Care

360 Cabs was founded in 2019 by a group of travel enthusiasts from Chennai who were frustrated by unreliable cabs, surprise charges, and unsafe rides. We set out to build something different — a taxi company built on transparency, safety, and genuine care for passengers.

Starting with just 5 cabs serving the Chennai–Pondicherry corridor, we've grown to a fleet of 50+ vehicles covering 150+ cities across Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Karnataka. Every driver in our team is hand-picked, trained, and treated as a partner — not just an employee.
